## About

CS student from Lahore, Pakistan - building **MoodCutter**, an AI-powered multimodal emotion detection engine for video content. MoodCutter reads the full emotional signature of a video - laughter in the audio, energy in the voice, expression on the face - and automatically clips the moments that make people feel something.

Focused on systems-level understanding and mathematical foundations before specialization. Building in public.

---

## What I'm Building

### 🎬 MoodCutter

> *"Other tools clip what you said. MoodCutter clips what you felt."*

A multimodal AI virality engine for content creators. Six-signal emotion detection pipeline: speech sentiment, laughter detection, voice intensity, facial emotion, crowd reaction, and a fusion layer that combines all signals into a single Virality Score with plain-language reasoning.

No competitor does this. Existing tools - OpusClip, TuBoost - detect what you said. MoodCutter detects what you felt.

| Repository | Status | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [MoodCutter](https://github.com/axtheon/MoodCutter) | Archived ✅ | POC - audio-based emotional moment detection |

| MoodCutter-Pro | Private 🔒 | Full product - six signals, hosted, coming soon |

**Stack:** Python · WhisperX · PyTorch · HuggingFace · librosa · OpenCV · FastAPI
