## About Me

I'm a CS student from Lahore, Pakistan, completing ICS while building
**MoodCutter** — an AI-powered emotional intelligence engine for video
that automatically detects and clips emotionally resonant moments using
multimodal signal detection: speech sentiment, laughter, voice intensity,
and facial emotion.

My approach prioritizes mathematical foundations and systems-level
understanding over surface-level tooling — the kind of depth that scales.
Currently progressing: ICS → BSCS, with deliberate emphasis on core CS
fundamentals before specialization.

---

## What I'm Building

### 🎬 MoodCutter
> *"Other tools clip what you said. MoodCutter clips what you felt."*

An AI virality engine for content creators. Instead of transcript-based
clipping like every competitor, MoodCutter reads the full emotional
signature of a video — laughter in the audio, energy spikes in the voice,
expressions on the face — and predicts why a moment will go viral.

- **Status:** POC complete (archived) · Full product in private development
- **Stack:** Python · WhisperX · PyTorch · HuggingFace · librosa · OpenCV
- **Repo:** [github.com/axtheon/MoodCutter](https://github.com/axtheon/MoodCutter)
