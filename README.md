## MoodCutter

Every AI video clipper reads transcripts. They find your most quotable line.
MoodCutter finds your most emotional moment.

Laughter in the audio. Energy in your voice. Expression on your face.
Six signals. One score. The clip that actually goes viral.

| Repository | Status | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [MoodCutter](https://github.com/axtheon/MoodCutter) | Archived | POC, audio-based emotional moment detection |
| [MoodCutter-Community](https://github.com/axtheon/MoodCutter-Community) | Active | Open source, three-signal pipeline, free, self-hosted |
| MoodCutter-Pro | Private | Full product, six signals, virality score, hosted |

**Stack:** Python · WhisperX · PyTorch · HuggingFace · librosa · OpenCV · FastAPI
